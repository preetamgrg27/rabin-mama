Clients are encouraged to book appointments in advance to secure their preferred time slots. We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

We strive to provide the best service possible. If you are not satisfied with your haircut or service, please inform us within 48 hours, and we will make necessary adjustments.

For the safety of our clients and staff, we maintain strict hygiene standards. We ask clients to inform us of any health conditions that may affect the service.

4. Payments and Refunds

Payments are due at the time of service. We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments. Refunds are not provided for services rendered but adjustments can be made if necessary.
