Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Do I need to book an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to secure your preferred time slot.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments for your convenience.

3. Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment with at least 24 hours' notice to avoid any fees.

4. Do you offer beard grooming services?

Yes, we offer a range of grooming services, including beard trimming, shaping, and maintenance.

5. Are your tools sanitized?

Absolutely! We maintain strict hygiene standards and sanitize all tools and equipment after each use.

6. Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome. However, booking an appointment is recommended during peak hours.

7. What is your refund policy?

We do not offer refunds for services rendered, but we are happy to make adjustments if needed.
