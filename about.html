About RB Barber Shop

Our Story

Welcome to RB Barber Shop, your premier barbershop located at Pokhara-1, Harichowk. Owned and operated by Rabin Gadal, a highly experienced senior barber with a passion for modern and traditional haircuts. We pride ourselves in offering a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere where each client is treated with the utmost care and attention. Our mission is to ensure you leave our shop looking and feeling your best.

Our Team

Rabin Gadal - Senior Barber & Owner

With over a decade of experience in the barbering industry, Rabin specializes in classic cuts, modern styles, and precision grooming. His attention to detail and commitment to excellence have built him a loyal client base. Rabin believes that a great haircut is not just about style, but also about the experience and confidence it brings.

Amrit - Junior Barber

Amrit is a talented junior barber with a growing reputation for quality cuts and attention to detail. Under the guidance of Rabin, he is learning the fine art of barbering, mastering various styles and techniques. His enthusiasm for the craft and dedication to customer satisfaction make him a valuable part of the team.
